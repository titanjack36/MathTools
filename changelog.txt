MathTools V1.2 (2019-02-02)
- Added functionality to Functions package:
    - Differentiation for all functions
    - Ability to perform basic simplification for sum and geometric functions
    - Improved the function printing format to be simpler

MathTools V1.1 (2019-01-23)
- Added functionality to FunctionConstructor expression parser:
	- Can now parse trigonometric, logarithmic and square root functions, and math constants
- Fixed bugs:
	- An expression with subtraction getting converted into addition
	- Unable to represent negative numbers
	- Dividing by negative denominator will register as subtraction
	- A negative exponent will register as subtraction
